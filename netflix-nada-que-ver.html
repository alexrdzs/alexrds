We set out to revamp Netflix Mexico's first podcast with a clear goal in mind: when you listen to this podcast, you'll see Netflix in a whole new way. So we put together the ultimate guide to finding and discovering new shows and movies on Netflix. Starring Javi Ibarreche, Plaqueta, and Luisa Iglesias, NQV dives into the latest releases, uncovers hidden gems, and shares fun facts and stories you didn't know.
